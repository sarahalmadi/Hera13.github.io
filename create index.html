Caesar Salad

  • Time: 15 minutes
  • Serves: 2 people
Ingredients Quantity
Romaine lettuce 1 head
croutons 1 cup
Parmesan cheese1/4 cup

Steps:

  1. wash and chop the lettuce
  2. Add croutons and cheese
  3. pour dressing and mix

Pizza

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 4people
Ingredients Quantity
Pizza dough 1 base
Tomato sauce 1/2 cup
Mozzarella cheese 1 cup
Toppings As desired

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven
  2. Spread sauce on dough
  3. Add cheese and toppings
  4. Bake until golden
